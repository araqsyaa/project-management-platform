### "Why Your App Over Competitors?"

1. **Jira is overkill for your needs**
   - Built for enterprise software teams
   - Overwhelming feature set
   - Steep learning curve
   - Expensive

2. **Trello is too simple**
   - Only kanban board
   - No task depth
   - Minimal progress tracking
   - Not scalable to real projects

3. **Asana is complex AND expensive**
   - Requires training
   - Feature-rich but confusing
   - Subscription model
   - Designed for large teams

4. **ClickUp tries to do everything**
   - Feature overload
   - Difficult UI
   - Bloated for small teams
   - Moderate-high cost

5. **Your App is the Goldilocks solution**
   - ✓ Just right for target users
   - ✓ All essential features
   - ✓ Zero complexity overhead
   - ✓ Self-hosted and transparent
   - ✓ Educational value

## For Your Diploma Presentation

### Recommended Narrative

> "Rather than try to compete with established enterprise tools, my application focuses on a specific problem: delivering professional project management to teams that don't need enterprise complexity. By targeting students and small teams, I've created a solution that is immediately useful, easy to understand, and technologically transparent."

### Why This Positioning Works

✓ **Realistic**: You can't outspend Jira or Asana on features  
✓ **Smart**: You've identified an underserved market  
✓ **Defensible**: Your simplicity is intentional, not accidental  
✓ **Educational**: You understand both the problem and the tradeoffs  
✓ **Professional**: Shows mature product thinking

### What To Emphasize

1. **Design Philosophy**: "Simplicity through constraint"
2. **Technical Depth**: Full-stack implementation
3. **User Focus**: Built for your actual target users
4. **Architectural Decisions**: Why React + Spring Boot + PostgreSQL
5. **Self-Hosting**: Data control and privacy
6. **Modern Stack**: Current industry best practices
